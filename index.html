HOW TO PLAY

  1. Stand up. Hold the phone in both hands like a bat handle, screen facing you.
  2. Read the scout report. Before each ball you see the bowler, speed, where it pitches, and which way it moves.
  3. Pick your shot. DEFEND protects your wicket, DRIVE finds gaps, SLOG goes for the fence but risks everything.
  4. Tap I'M READY. The bowler starts his run-up. About four seconds later the ball is coming at you.
  5. Swing right after the bounce. You'll hear a thock when it pitches. That sound is your trigger. Too early or too late is a clean miss.
  6. Swing through the ball's line. The gyro reads your real swing: a flat horizontal swipe is a pull (leg side) or cut (off side), an up-and-over swing is a straight drive. Pull a ball that's wide outside off and you will hit air.
  7. Find the gaps. Ring fielders stop anything hit straight at them.
